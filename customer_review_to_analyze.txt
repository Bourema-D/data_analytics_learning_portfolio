
I purchased this project management software after reading several positive reviews and watching the promotional videos on the company's website. Based on the advertised features, I expected a reliable, intuitive, and efficient tool that would help my team organize tasks, manage deadlines, and improve collaboration. Unfortunately, my experience has been the exact opposite, and after using the software for several weeks, I am extremely disappointed with both the product and the overall customer experience.

The installation process was more complicated than expected. Even though the documentation claimed that setup could be completed in just a few minutes, I encountered multiple installation errors that required several attempts before the software finally launched. The user guide did not address the issues I encountered, and the troubleshooting section was too generic to be useful. I eventually had to search online forums to find temporary solutions that should have been included in the official documentation.

Once the software was installed, I immediately noticed that the user interface felt outdated and cluttered. Basic actions such as creating a new project, assigning tasks, or generating reports required navigating through multiple menus. Instead of simplifying our workflow, the software made routine tasks more complicated. Several buttons were poorly labeled, some settings were difficult to locate, and the dashboard provided far too much unnecessary information while hiding the options we actually needed.

Performance has been one of the biggest disappointments. The application frequently freezes when working with larger projects, and loading times become painfully slow whenever multiple users are connected simultaneously. Saving changes often takes several seconds, and on more than one occasion the application became completely unresponsive, forcing me to restart it. After restarting, some of the changes my team had made were missing, which created confusion and required us to repeat work that had already been completed.

Synchronization between devices has also been unreliable. One of the main reasons we selected this software was the promise of seamless cloud synchronization across desktop and mobile devices. Unfortunately, updates made on one device often fail to appear on another for several minutes or even hours. Sometimes tasks disappear temporarily before reappearing later, making it difficult to know whether information has actually been saved. This lack of consistency has reduced our confidence in the platform and forced us to verify every update manually.

The notification system is equally frustrating. Team members either receive duplicate notifications for the same event or receive no notification at all. Deadlines have been missed simply because reminders were never delivered. At other times, the application sends alerts hours after a task has already been completed. A productivity tool should improve communication, not create additional uncertainty.

Another major concern is the search functionality. Searching for projects, files, or comments produces inconsistent results. Keywords that clearly exist in project descriptions often fail to return any matches, while unrelated projects sometimes appear at the top of the results list. This makes it unnecessarily difficult to locate important information, especially when managing a large number of projects.

The reporting features were another reason we purchased the software, but they have been equally disappointing. Reports take a long time to generate, and the exported files frequently contain formatting errors. Charts occasionally display incorrect values, forcing us to manually verify the data before presenting it to management. This completely defeats the purpose of having automated reporting tools.

Security and reliability have also become concerns. The software unexpectedly logged users out several times during the workday, and session timeouts occurred even while users were actively working. On one occasion, the application displayed an error message indicating that it could not verify user credentials, despite the credentials being correct. These interruptions significantly reduced productivity and raised concerns about the stability of the authentication system.

Customer support has unfortunately been the worst part of the experience. I contacted technical support several times regarding installation issues, synchronization failures, and performance problems. Responses were consistently slow, often taking several days to arrive. When support finally replied, the responses appeared to be copied from a standard template and rarely addressed the specific issue I had described. In several cases I was simply directed to documentation that I had already consulted before opening the support ticket.

Software updates have not improved the situation. While new versions are released regularly, they often introduce new bugs while failing to resolve existing ones. After one update, the application's performance became noticeably slower, and another update caused several keyboard shortcuts to stop working. It feels as though updates are released without sufficient testing.

Considering the subscription price, I expected a much higher level of quality. There are many competing products that offer a cleaner interface, faster performance, more reliable synchronization, and significantly better customer support for a similar or even lower cost. Instead of helping our team become more productive, this software has created additional work, increased frustration, and wasted valuable time.

Overall, I cannot recommend this product in its current state. While the concept behind the software is promising, the execution falls far short of expectations. The frequent bugs, poor performance, unreliable synchronization, confusing interface, and ineffective customer support make it difficult to justify the investment. Unless substantial improvements are made to stability, usability, and customer service, I will be canceling our subscription and migrating to another platform. I genuinely wanted this software to succeed, but after weeks of continuous issues and disappointing support, I have lost confidence in both the product and the company behind it.
